## Visual Design

**References:** scale.com + raycast.com hybrid

### Color Palette
- **Background:** Near-black with a subtle blue tint (~#070921 or similar deep navy-black)
- **Text:** Greyish white (not pure white — softer, like #B0B8C8 for body, #E2E8F0 for headings)
- **Accent:** Purple/violet (~#8B5CF6 range) for CTAs, highlights, and interactive elements
- **Cards/surfaces:** Slightly lighter dark (#0F1629 range) for subtle contrast

### Typography
- Rounded, highly readable sans-serif (candidates: Inter, Plus Jakarta Sans, Nunito Sans, or Geist)
- Large, confident headings
- Generous line height for body text

### Layout & Navigation
- **Sticky top bar** with section links — clicking smooth-scrolls to the corresponding section
- Sections: Services | Clients | About | Speaking | Contact
- Logo/name on the left, nav links on the right
- Hero section is full viewport height, value proposition front and center

### Hero Treatment
- Short, punchy value proposition immediately visible — catches the eye before any scrolling
- Large headline + subheadline + CTA buttons
- Credibility bar (Google, Amazon, Meta) below the fold or subtly in hero
- Possible subtle abstract/geometric background element (gradient mesh, grid pattern, or animated particles)

### Visual Elements
- Abstract/geometric — no stock photos
- Subtle gradients or glow effects for depth
- Clean card-based layout for services and audience sections
- Icons for service cards (line-style, matching the rounded aesthetic)
