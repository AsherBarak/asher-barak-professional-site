My Story

From Code to Strategy and Back Again

I started my career as a software engineer and never really stopped being one - even as I moved into leadership roles at some of the world's biggest tech companies. I've always been the kind of leader who wants to understand the technology deeply, not just manage it from a distance.

At Amazon, I led the team reimagining Alexa Shopping with AI. At Meta, I drove AI adoption across engineering teams. At Google, I worked on large-scale systems that serve billions. Along the way I picked up an economics degree and a deep appreciation for where technology meets business reality.

Today I work independently, helping companies navigate the AI landscape. I do strategy when they need direction, get hands-on when they need building, and speak publicly about what I've learned along the way.
